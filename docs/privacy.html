Privacy Policy

Wodcard is an offline-first app. All entries you log are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to us or any third party. We do not collect, track, or sell any personal data.

The only network activity in this app is Apple's own StoreKit purchase and subscription verification system, used to unlock the optional Pro upgrade. We never see your payment details; that is handled entirely by Apple.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is deleted with it.
